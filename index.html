About Us 关于我们

English Introduction

Qingyuan Hongyang Trading Co., Ltd is a professional new and used automobile export enterprise registered in Guangdong. We supply BYD, Toyota, Nissan, Mercedes and other vehicles, provide full set export service including inspection, customs clearance and global shipping, serve Southeast Asia, Central Asia, Africa and Latin America, support LHD & RHD cars.
