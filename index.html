About Us 关于我们

English Introduction

Qingyuan Hongyang Trading Co., Ltd is a professional new and used automobile export enterprise registered in Qingyuan, Guangdong Province. We focus on global automobile export business, covering mainstream fuel vehicles, new energy EV, pickup trucks, off-road vehicles and commercial vans from BYD, Toyota, Nissan, BMW, Mercedes, Changan, MG and other well-known brands.

We have stable vehicle supply channels, sufficient spot inventory, and provide full one-stop export services including vehicle condition inspection, document handling, customs declaration, port delivery and global sea freight. Our products are exported to Curacao, Algeria, Mexico, Central Asia, Southeast Asia and other regions, supporting left-hand drive and right-hand drive vehicles.

We have professional multilingual sales team online 24/7 via WhatsApp, reply customer inquiries quickly, provide real car photos, videos and detailed vehicle reports for every client.


企业简介

清远市鸿洋商贸有限公司是广东清远正规注册专业新旧汽车出口企业，主营全球整车出口业务，主营比亚迪、丰田、日产、宝马、奔驰、长安、名爵等主流品牌燃油车、新能源电车、皮卡、越野车。

公司拥有稳定车源渠道、充足现车库存，提供全车检测、单证办理、报关、港口交运、全球海运一站式全套出口服务，车辆远销库拉索、阿尔及利亚、墨西哥、中亚、东南亚等多国，支持左舵、右舵车型出口。

配备专业多语种外贸销售团队，WhatsApp全天候在线，快速回复海外客户询价，每台车型均可提供实拍图片、视频、完整车况检测报告。
